Frequently asked questions

Is there a beta version of the platform you're developing?

The beta version of IdeaFTW will be released on the third quarter of this year 2022. For now, you can follow our socials to see sneak peeks of our prototypes!

Can I buy FTW token?

We aim to raise funds for ideaFTW through our token sale which will be available to buy in the near future. The token will be listed on centralised and decentralised exchanges. Further information cannot be disclosed. Good news are comming!

How many rewards will i receive?

The main feature and business model in which IdeaFTW is based on is on rewarding its users while maintaining a steady cash-flow through the SaaS that we provide to companies. Users will be rewarded uniquely with FTW tokens once their idea is posted, meaning that depending on the amount of likes, they will be rewarded more or less FTW tokens. The token will be available to exchange for USDT and USDC at CEX and DEX.

Are there any requirements to post or receive rewards for my ideas?

Signing up is as easy as connecting your browser integrated wallet (ex: metamask) to the platform of IdeaFTW. Once the wallet is connected, you can create posts, go up on level and claim your rewards.
